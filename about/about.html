We're more than just
Asian mental health.

in-between talks Asian mental health, but our conversations go further.

Students, raise your hands if you've heard this line before. (don't worry, this is only worth 10% of your grade.)

In-between agrees. We, the youth, are the best shot our future has.

To us, though, we're asking a different question.

Are we going to do great things in a healthier, more effective way than those who came before us?

lol. we don't think so. (at least, not yet.)

the problem

we're entering a world more complicated than any of us can imagine; exponential change is happening technologically, politically, culturally, literally, every day.

on the other hand, we are being tasked with the keys to a very, very expensive car called "the future." the car needs a very good wash. and new tires. and an oil change. also, the roof is gone. also, does anyone have an air freshener?

also, we do not know how to drive!! we're busy enough picking a major, launching our careers, remembering to eat lunch, juggling three meal points, meeting intrinsic and extrinsic demands, coping with twenty-six deadlines, and forgetting to sleep.

are we really expecting ourselves to be... okay?
